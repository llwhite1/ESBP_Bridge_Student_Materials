# Bridge Student Release Policy

Current workbooks and notebooks are available before the corresponding class work. Once a daily activity is complete, its solutions are published on the next business day in two phases:

- 2:30 PM Central: current workbook key and available legacy workbook keys.
- 4:00 PM Central: current student quiz, current quiz key, and available legacy quiz files/keys.

Days 1-8 are already released. Days 10-16 follow the checked schedule. Day 9 Exam 1 and Day 17 Exam 2 are explicit non-release boundaries.

The automation copies only PDF files named in the schedule, verifies their SHA-256 hashes, updates the public ledger/status, regenerates the manifest, and runs repository validation before Git publication.
